# Community Merger Report

### **Summary of Action**
Total Master Communities Before Merge: **1,650**
Total Master Communities After Merge: **1,461**
**Net Duplicates Removed & Merged: 189**

*Note: All contract values (Monthly/Annual) for the merged properties were summed together. All Service History logs previously pointing to the duplicate records were successfully rewired to the remaining Primary Master Record.*

---

### **Part 1: "Fuzzy" Variations Merged**
Before the grand merge, the following property names had slightly different spellings or typos. I standardized the names of the duplicates to match the Master Name, which allowed them to be merged in Part 2.
